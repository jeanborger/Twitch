What's a twitch?

A twitch is a tiny snippet of thought. An impulse. An unchecked response to the stimuli, inner and outer, that bombard us every day.

A twitch is a chain of words that come to us when we let words come.

Poets shape their poems, but a twitch by its very nature is unshaped. It's a blurt. It's not something we craft, but something we release, and perhaps later, try to shape or understand.

A twitch is elusive, but utterly natural. It's the art of an instant. Art for all. Instant ART.
