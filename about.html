What's a twitch?

A twitch is an impulse. A blurt. A chain of words that comes to us when we let words come.

It's a slip of thought. A thought that slips out. An unchecked response to our inner and outer worlds.

Poets shape their poems, but a twitch is unshaped. It's not something we craft, but something we release.

Sometimes elusive, always beyond our control, a twitch is the art of an instant. Art for all. Instant ART.

Why twitch?

Twitch because you can. Because you care. Twitch because it helps you in some way.

In our fast-paced world, our minds are constantly being pulled in mutltiple directions, this way and that.

In the flux of stimuli, something is lost. Our ability to tune in. To relax into creativity. To create just for the sake of creating.

A twitch says, at this moment, what is bubbling up inside me? Is it something sacred? Something mundane?

Let it flash onto the screen. Onto the page. Out into the world. Let it be known. If only to yourself. If only for an instant.
