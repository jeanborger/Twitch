What's a twitch?

A twitch is an impulse. A blurt. A chain of words that comes to us when we let words come.

A twitch is a slip of thought. A thought that slips out. An unchecked response to inner and outer worlds.

Poets shape their poems, but a twitch is unshaped. It's not something we craft, but something we release.

A twitch is sometimes elusive, always beyond our control. The art of an instant. Art for all. Instant ART.

Why twitch?

Twitch because you can. Because you care. Because it helps you in some way.
